In this article, we explore how to implement a complete commercial-financial cycle automation using Salesforce as the central platform.

## The Challenge

Companies operating across multiple countries face a critical challenge: keeping the sales cycle in sync with electronic invoicing, each country with its own fiscal regulations.

## The Solution

We implemented an architecture based on:

- **Apex Triggers** for post-contract signing process automation
- **Lightning Web Components** for the approval and review interface
- **Salesforce Flows** to orchestrate the multi-country invoicing pipeline

## Results

- 80% reduction in invoice issuance time
- Zero fiscal validation errors thanks to automated business rules
- Complete cycle unified on a single platform
